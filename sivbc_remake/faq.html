FAQ!

Our Answers To General Questions About Club


What is club volleyball?

Club volleyball is much like other select sports. There are open tryouts for team selections after which teams will practice 2-3 times per week and play a tournament approximately every other weekend for 4 – 7 months depending on the age group.

What are the age group definitions for 22-23 season?

More details on age group definitions can be found by reviewing the USA Volleyball

Junior Player Age Definitions. Age group definitions for 2022-23 season are as follows:

U18 – Players born on or after July 1, 2005 or Player born on or after July 2004 and a high school student in the twelfth grade.

U17 – Players born on or after July 1, 2006

U16 – Players born on or after July 1, 2007

U15 – Players born on or after July 1, 2008

U14 – Players born on or after July 1, 2009

U13 – Players born on or after July 1, 2010

U12 – Players born on or after July 1, 2011

U11 – Players born on or after July 1, 2012

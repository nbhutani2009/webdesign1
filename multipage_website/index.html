Let's start with the 3 most common ways people play volleyball.

1. Indoor Volleyball

Indoor volleyball is the most common version of volleyball and the kind that people think of when they hear the word. Indoor volleyball is played in a gym, with a court that is 30 feet long, with six players on the court at one time. Pros: More people on the court, feet free to move without anything holding them back, not affected by weather or sunlight, AC indoors, softer Cons: Less touches, loud arena

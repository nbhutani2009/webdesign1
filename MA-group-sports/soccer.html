Interview with Soccer Players!!

We have met with a couple of Soccer players at our school and this is what they had to say about tryouts:

How do tryouts work?

  • How does the tryout start and end?
    •
  • The practice starts with a basic athletic warm-up, and you start with simple moves like shooting goals, 1 on 1s, etc.
  • Then you move on to scrimmiging, and you play in your posistions and scrimmage in seperate positions
  • During the tryout, they pull several kids to offer them a spot, and for the rest of the tryout you continue scrimminging
  • What should you do to prep for tryouts?
    •
  • First you should start by keeping consistency, and practicing dribbling, kicking and other drills in your free time will help you prep well.
  • Its important to practice throwing and catching, also known as your fundamentals of ultimate
  • Practice also defensive drills and offensive drills depending on the position
  • Finding a partner would also be great! You can practice several drills together, and even play 1 on 1s for real game experience.
  • Note: Even if you play a specific posistion, make sure you're getting practice around all skills!
